Needtobreathe

Live From the Woods

Needtobreathe

Release Date: April 14th, 2015


Recorded live on September 13th, 2014, in Carl Black Chevy Woods Amphitheater, Whites Creek, Tennessee, this album has been described by Rolling Stone as
"A renaissance album, years ahead of its time as Needtobreathe artfully managed to evoke
rock and roll with southern charm. My soul was awoken to soulful, country blues".

Band Members

  • "Bear" Rinehart III-Lead vocals, guitar, piano, organ and harmonica
  • Bo Rinehart-Lead guitar, banjo, mandolin and backing vocals
  • Seth Bolt-Bass, mandolin, percussion, drums and backing vocals
  • Josh Lovelace-Hammond organ, piano and backing vocals
